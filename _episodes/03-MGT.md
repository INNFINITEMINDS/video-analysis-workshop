## Introduction to MGT


MGT has two main functionalities: 

- dynamic visualisations (video files)
	- motion video
	- motion history video
- static visualisations (images)
	- motion images
	- motion history images
	- motiongrams
	- videograms
- motion data (csv files)
	- quantity of motion
	- centroid of motion
	- area of motion


There are also several support functions.
